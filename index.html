Isochrone Map

Understanding how long it takes to travel from one point to another is essential for students. Isochrone maps show areas that can be reached within a certain time frame, helping you visualize your commute and plan your day more efficiently. Use this map to explore travel times from various locations in London.

Housing related

Finding the right place to live is a crucial part of your student experience. London offers a wide range of housing options, from university dormitories to private rentals. Consider factors such as proximity to your university, budget, and the type of accommodation that suits your lifestyle.

- Consider proximity to your university.
- Check the average rental prices in the area.
- Look for nearby amenities like grocery stores and public transport.
- Visit the neighborhood at different times of the day to get a feel for the area.
- Read reviews from other students who have lived there.
